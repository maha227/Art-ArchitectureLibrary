About this Job 

Art & Architecture Library Information Assistants help the Architecture Library visitors find information and help ensure the library runs smoothly. Assists in the daily operations of the library in Education Hall, Rm. 101 on the Long Island Campus of New York Tech.  

You will get valuable experience working with a small team, and interacting with both students, faculty, and staff. You’ll improve your research skills, and apply classroom knowledge, especially architectural history and design research. Depending on your interests and our workload, there are additional opportunities to work on projects such as virtual exhibitions and library workshops. 

 

## Library Student Assistant Responsibilities: 

Encourage use of a variety of library collections, services, and events and assist library users in locating library and online information sources. 

* Help fellow students search databases and locate print and electronic resources in a variety of books, and journals by following the search results and citations.  

* Shelving library books, scanning; printing, refilling printer paper, operating the wide format scanner and mediascape table, cleaning the wide format scanner and flatbed scanner, and assisting visitors with minor software needs.  

* Assist library users with lending tech including virtual reality headsets and recording microphones. 

* Assist in the design of digital and print marketing materials, virtual exhibitions for library space and events. 

* Contribute to semester-long research projects such as GIS skills training, and anti-racism collections.
