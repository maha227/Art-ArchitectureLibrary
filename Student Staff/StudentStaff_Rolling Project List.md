## New York Tech Historical Digitized Photography Collection – Omeka Collection 

## Goal: 
Build a collection in the New York Tech repository. The collection will include a series of New York Tech historical photographs which were originally collected and digitized by the Communications Department of the College of Arts & Science. The meta-data should meet standards required by the New York State Empire Collection. Additional meta-data could include geotagging locations for broader connection to the campus history.
