Innovation Lab Weather Station

The Innovation Lab Weather Station is a real-time environmental monitoring system that collects and displays local weather data such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, precipitation, and atmospheric pressure. It is used as both a research and teaching tool within the lab to support experimentation, data analysis, and design-related studies connected to environmental conditions and the built environment.

This station allows students to engage directly with live environmental data while learning how weather information can inform architectural design, sustainability strategies, and research projects.
